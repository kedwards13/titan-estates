Our Services

Quick Cash Close

At Titan Estates, we can close on your house in as little as 7 days. This process eliminates the need for financing contingencies, bypasses appraisal and inspection contingencies, and provides instant cash, making it ideal for sellers in difficult situations like divorce or job loss. Our streamlined approach reduces paperwork and removes the risk of a deal falling through due to financing issues, allowing you to move quickly and with peace of mind.

Seller Finance

Our seller financing options enable you to cash flow your mortgage, offering benefits such as faster closings and avoiding commission fees. This method allows sellers to avoid traditional financing hurdles, making the process smoother and often quicker. By offering financing to buyers, sellers can sometimes achieve a higher selling price and attract a broader range of buyers.

Crypto Transactions

We offer the option to buy or sell houses using cryptocurrency on the blockchain. This method provides transparency and security for both buyers and sellers. Transactions are processed quickly and can reduce the costs associated with traditional real estate transactions. Using blockchain technology, we ensure a seamless transfer of property ownership that benefits both parties.
